Section 01
Information We Collect

When you use InsureAI, we collect certain information to provide accurate premium predictions and a personalised experience. This includes information you provide directly and data collected automatically.

Personal Information you provide:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number during registration
  • Age, gender, and date of birth for premium calculation
  • BMI, smoking status, and number of dependents
  • Geographic region for regional risk assessment
  • Payment details (processed securely via third-party gateways)

Automatically collected data:

  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Referral source and session identifiers
💡 We only collect information that is strictly necessary for delivering our services. You may choose not to provide optional fields, though this may affect prediction accuracy.
⚙️
Section 02
How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us provide, improve, and personalise the InsureAI platform. We use it for the following purposes:

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis
Insurance premium prediction Age, BMI, smoker status, region Contract performance
Account creation & management Name, email, phone Contract performance
Service improvements & AI model training Anonymised usage data Legitimate interest

We do not use your data to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects without human oversight.

🤝
Section 03
Data Sharing & Third Parties

InsureAI does never sell your personal data to any third party. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Insurance Partners: With your explicit consent, anonymised risk profiles may be shared with licensed insurers to generate real plan quotes.
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist in hosting, payments, and analytics — all bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation under the IT Act 2000 or applicable Indian law.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data will be transferred subject to the same privacy protections.
🔒 All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to process your data only for the specified purpose and under strict confidentiality obligations.
🔐
Section 04
Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

  • All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (256-bit encryption)
  • Sensitive fields such as health data are stored with AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted quarterly
  • Two-factor authentication is available and recommended for all accounts
  • Incident response procedures are in place to notify users within 72 hours of any breach

While we take every precaution, no system is 100% immune. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable 2FA on your account.

Section 05
Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyse platform usage. Cookies we use include:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Login session, security tokens Session
Preference Cookies Language, theme, region settings 1 year
Analytics Cookies Page views, usage behaviour (anonymised) 90 days
Marketing Cookies Personalised offers (with consent only) 30 days

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Section 06
Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw marketing consent at any time without affecting prior processing
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer or the relevant authority
📩 To exercise any of the above rights, email us at privacy@insureai.in with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.
🗂️
Section 07
Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

  • Active account data is retained for the duration of your account
  • After account deletion, personal data is purged within 30 days
  • Anonymised usage data may be retained indefinitely for model improvement
  • Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as per Indian tax law
  • Backup copies are securely deleted within 90 days of the primary deletion
👶
Section 08
Children's Privacy

InsureAI is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@insureai.in. We will promptly delete such information from our records.

Section 09
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our business practices. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify registered users via email at least 14 days before changes take effect
  • Display a prominent notice on the platform dashboard

Continued use of InsureAI after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
