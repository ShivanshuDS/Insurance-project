Why We Built This

Insurance shouldn't be a black box

We built InsureAI as a final year ML project — but the problem it solves is real. Millions of people overpay for insurance simply because they don't understand how premiums are calculated.

🔍
Full Transparency
We show you exactly which factors affect your premium and by how much.
🤝
No Hidden Agenda
We don't sell your data or push specific plans. This tool is purely educational.
🎓
Built for Learning
Every part of our pipeline — from data cleaning to model deployment — is documented.
The Process

How InsureAI Works

📥
Data Collection
1,338 real insurance records collected with age, BMI, gender, region, smoking status & charges.
🧹
Data Cleaning
Duplicates removed, categorical features encoded — gender, smoker, region mapped to numeric values.
🤖
Model Training
Linear Regression trained on 80% data with sklearn. Achieved 97% R² accuracy on test set.
⚡
Live Prediction
Enter your details, get instant premium estimate with full breakdown — in under 1 second.
